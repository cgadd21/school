When the sun sets guests experience EPCOT Forever. This event celebrates the past, present, and future. Fun fact, when I was working in sales at Apple, I would show customers this event on my phone. This showcases how well the camera was able to capture nightlife. After showing, it was an easy sell. Worked every time.

Fireworks

This show features fireworks and special effects. This was taken in "China". Each section in EPOCOT is based on a Country. Fun fact, China invented fireworks. Ironic isn't it.

Lights

The park lights up to give another view of the attractions. I originally thought the pyramid was the Bass Pro Shops at the Pyramid.

Ball

The giant EPCOT ball drowns in color as the night begins. During the day, it looks like a giant golf ball. Inside this massive structure is the Spaceship Earth attraction.
